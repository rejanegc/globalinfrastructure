Our Mission

“… To create a better everyday life for our customers in the IBM company. Customers will never love our platform service until we love it .”

Kevin Ingwersen, Platform Lead

  1. Grow to a best in industry on premise platform service deploying cloud capabilities where appropriate .
  2. Provide current, enterprise class data centre and hardware solutions capable of scaling to customer demands
  3. Deploy a single global virtualization solution capable of handling failures at the physical layer                
  4. Enable a single entry point for our customers to dynamically engage with our services                            
  5. Leverage commercial GTS & GBS services where appropriate                                                                               
  6. Consolidate to a small number of strategic physical locations                                                                                
  7. Model ourselves after “whats best” in the industry – Don’t hesitate to look at cloud providers for examples!
  8. Deliver just a few standardized services, focusing on delivering them extremely well                                                                          
  9. The leadership team will set the vision – the squads drive to that vision via continuous improvement to our services and sun-setting the old, starting the new
